    About Me

    Hello there!! I’m Namratha, or Nam for short. I’m an aspiring Data Analyst eager to launch a career in the field of Data Analytics.

    I live in the music city of the US - Nashville, TN and I’m originally from Mysore, a city in the Southern Indian state of Karnataka which is also referred to as the “City of Palaces”.

    I have a background in Electrical Engineering and Web development. I was always curious about the world of data, processes and practices of Data Analytics. My curiosity to learn more and my passion for coding led me to take the Google Certification Course on Data Analytics which opened me to the world of data. I thoroughly enjoyed the experience of working with data, transforming it, analyzing it and creating impactful visualizations from data findings. It further solidified my passion for coding and watching it come to life.

    I look forward to further harnessing my technical and social skills to make a difference in real world problems with creative technology solutions.

    When I’m not coding, you can find me in the gym getting a good work out in or at home spending quality time with my family. I am a big foodie! My favorite cuisine is Indian, followed by Thai and Italian. I love to try all kinds of food and also enjoy cooking for my family :)
